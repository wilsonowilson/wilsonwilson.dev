## What I'm doing now

Today (2022-04-01), I spent a good deal of time doing research about cold email. From the little I've learned, I understand that the cold email process can be broken down into three or more stages:

- Lead prospecting
- First contact
- Following up if there's no response

So I'll be going through this process in that order.
